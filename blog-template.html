Start with an opening paragraph that states the problem and why it matters. Keep it specific and grounded in the context of real sermon note-taking.

Suggested Post Structure

  • Opening: one pain point your reader feels during or after teaching.
  • Method: one clear workflow they can try this week.
  • Example: a concrete sample note layout or before/after snapshot.
  • Close: one action step and one reflection question.

Add Scripture references, screenshots, and short pull quotes where useful. Keep paragraphs short so the post stays skimmable on mobile.
